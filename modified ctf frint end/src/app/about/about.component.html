About CEG TECH FORUM....:)

"

The main aim of CEG Tech Forum is to create an informal community where CEG’s students can collaborate on ideas and projects. It is envisioned to be a place where there is no rigidity and where constant interaction between the seniors and juniors can be mutually beneficial. This can be achieved only by the combined efforts of both the student directors and the students. If someone has an idea and doesn’t know how to go about it, he/she can always come to the CTF for pointers. There might not be anyone who is an expert in that domain, but we can always direct you to someone who might be able to help.

"
